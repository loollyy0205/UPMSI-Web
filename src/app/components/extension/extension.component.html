COMMUNITY OUTREACH
Summer Camps for marine science and climate change are held annually in partnership with the Department of Science and Technology Science Education Institute (DOST-SEI). Each year, 40 students and 20 teachers from public high schools are invited to join the camp so they can immerse and develop a deeper understanding of the importance of the oceans in our lives, especially as a maritime country.
