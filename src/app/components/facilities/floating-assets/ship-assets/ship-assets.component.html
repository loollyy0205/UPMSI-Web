MY PANATA

Of the three UP MSI vessels, RV Panata has participated in the most number of projects and expeditions. It has set sail in the West Philippine Sea (2019 & 2021), the Visayan Sea (2019), and the Tubbataha Reef (2019 & 2021).

RV KALAYAAN

The compact RV Kalayaan is the main vessel used by the Bolinao Marine Laboratory. It explores the waters of Pangasinan for sample collection and field experiments.

RV MANDARAGAT

RV Mandaragat is a specialized boat used primarily to transport the clay dispersal unit that controls harmful algal bloom (HAB) phenomena by removing HAB cells.
