FLOATING ASSETS

To increase the country’s capacity for ocean research, the UP SYSTEM operates research vessels for coastal and seasonal open ocean expeditions. Among them are the RV Kasarinlan and the RV Kalayaan. Their activities range from seismic surveys to oceanographic and plastics research.
