UP CENTER FOR RESEARCH AND AWARENESS OF DIVERSE LIVING ENVIRONMENTS

The Puerto Galera Biodiversity and Environmental Research and Outreach Center is envisioned as a world‐class research and outreach facility showcasing the Philippines’ remarkable biodiversity; enabling trans-disciplinary and synergistic collaboration between experts across UP campuses as well as local and foreign researchers and practitioners; and demonstrating the mutualism of a public‐private partnership.
