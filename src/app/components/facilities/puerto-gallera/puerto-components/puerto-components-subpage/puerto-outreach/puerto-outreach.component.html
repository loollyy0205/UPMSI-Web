Education & Conservation Classes

The University of the Philippines offers a variety of courses that can be held in whole or partly at the Puerto Galera facility. This venue would provide an opportunity for synergy and collaboration of the various units in offering courses. For example, team teaching can be developed that harnesses the strengths of experts from different units. New holistic and transdisciplinary courses can also be developed.

PG Station as Educational Facility

Harnessing the various expertise of the University of the Philippines, mini‐show areas can be set‐up as an educational facility for the locals, tourists and visitors in the facility.

  • Biodiversity exhibits
  • Research output exhibits
  • Puerto Galera ecosystems goods and services exhibit
  • Herb garden
  • Renewable energy for local communities
  • Native and endangered plants garden
