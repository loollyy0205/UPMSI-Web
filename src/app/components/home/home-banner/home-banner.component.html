NARFleet is on its way
to prowl Philippine waters

The National Academic Research Fleet (NARFleet) is set to embark on its first expedition soon. Established as a response to limited ocean research capacity, the NARFleet program aims to provide better access to ocean research for both the academe and the Philippine government. To achieve this, participating research groups will address problems such as reef protection and productivity, mapping the pelagic zone, and maximizing the use of bio resources.

The Bolinao Marine Laboratory

The Bolinao Marine Laboratory (BML) is the main research station of the UP Marine Science Institute and is one of the leading scientific institutions in Southeast Asia. The BML is located in the municipality of Bolinao, province of Pangasinan on the northern corner of the Lingayen Gulf in the northern Philippines.
