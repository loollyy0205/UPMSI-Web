Marine Natural Products Legacy Collection

The waters surrounding the archipelago are valuable resources to the Philippines. The UP Marine Science Institute empowers the marine sector by addressing various research gaps and by promoting the sustainable use of marine and coastal resources. From the surveillance of oceanic ecosystems to the development of marine-based technologies, the UPMSI designs and implements research programs that fall under the fields of ecology, oceanography, biology and biochemistry, geology, and conservation.
