LIBRARY
The UPMSI Library maintains and monitors a collection of books, scientific publications, theses and dissertations, audio-visual and cartographic materials, and reprints.
MUSEUM & EXHIBITS
One of the UPMSI objectives is to spread information and awareness about coastal and marine environments. The museum located within the UPMSI building complex showcases fish and corals and other invertebrates.
Marine Natural Products Legacy Collection
The UPMSI houses several exhibits that showcase marine flora and fauna collections. The exhibits feature locally- and internationally-procured specimens of fish, seaweeds, sea grass, corals, and microbes.
