MUSEUMS & EXHIBITS

One of the UP MSI objectives is to spread information and awareness about coastal and marine environments. The museum located within the UP MSI building complex showcases fish and corals and other invertebrates. Mobile exhibits on humanitarian and environmental topics like conservation are set up in universities and public spaces around the Philippines.
