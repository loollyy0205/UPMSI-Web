Resilient Coastal Livelihoods and Ecosystems

October 25, 2021

The webinar "Resilient Coastal Livelihoods and Ecosystems" was held on October 25, 2021 via Zoom and Facebook. This was conducted in cooperation with the National Committee on Marine Sciences (NCMS) of the UNESCO National Commission of the Philippines (UNACOM), in support of the UNESCO Decade of Ocean Science.
