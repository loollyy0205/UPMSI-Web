RESEARCH PROGRAMS & PROJECTS

Research is the primary role of the UP Marine Science Institute. Topics of interest include natural products, pollution, marine culture and productivity, genetics and genomics, and marine chemistry.

RESEARCH TYPE

Aside from contributing to the knowledge gaps in the science, UPMSI projects also aim to develop tools, facilities, and biobanks. These research endeavors are funded by the Department of Science and Technology, the Department of Agriculture, the UP Office of the Vice President for Academic Affairs, and the Commission on Higher Education.
