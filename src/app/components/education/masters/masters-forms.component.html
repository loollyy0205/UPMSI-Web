MASTER’S PROGRAM

The program leading to the degree of Master of Science in Marine Science (Marine Biology, Marine Physical Sciences, and Marine Biotechnology) aims to provide: (1) basic understanding of the nature of biological and physical processes in the ocean. 2) rigorous training formulating and carrying out independent research.

The program leading to the degree of Master of Science in Marine Science (Marine Biology, Marine Physical Sciences, and Marine Biotechnology) aims to provide: (1) basic understanding of the nature of biological and physical processes in the ocean. 2) rigorous training formulating and carrying out independent research.

The program

Graduate students must choose a subfield of marine science to specialize in. This will determine what kind of research they will be doing throughout the duration of their stay in the UP MSI.

Three major tracks are offered: marine biology, marine physical sciences, and marine biotechnology. Each specialization has unique set of core courses that lay the foundation of the student’s expertise

HOW TO APPLY

ADMISSION

• A high degree of intellectual capacity and aptitude for research and advanced studies

• A bachelor’s degree from a recognized institution of higher learning

• A weighted average of at least 2.25 or its equivalent in the natural sciences and mathematics

○ Physics (thermodynamics and motion) units

○ Calculus units

○ Organic chemistry units

○ General biology units

○ Elementary biochemistry is required for applicants in Marine Biotechnology

○ Geology 11 or its equivalent for those interested in Marine Physical Sciences

APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS

• A letter of application

• Curriculum vitae

• Two (2) duly accomplished application forms

• The original and duplicate copy of the undergraduate and graduate official transcript of records

• Duly accomplished recommendation forms from two former professors and/or supervisors.

○ Only 2 if both have doctorate degrees; 3 if only one has a doctorate degree, the other 2 must have master’s degree.

• Payment of application fees (non-refundable)

• For foreign applicants, whose native language and medium of instruction are not English, certification of English Proficiency (Test of English as a Foreign Language, TOEFL average score of 500)

• Satisfaction of additional University requirements such as a health clearance and other special admission requirements that may be imposed by a Graduate Committee.

Completed applications shall be submitted to the Graduate Office of the College of Science. The Institute’s Graduate Committee evaluates applications and endorses acceptance to the Dean.

REQUIREMENTS FOR THE MSc DEGREE

• Approved Program of Study

• At least 24 units of formal graduate courses

○ 12 units of electives in either the biological, physical sciences or biotechnology and have his or her thesis in the corresponding discipline

• Cumulative Weighted Average Grade (CWAG) of not lower than 2.0 for the duration of the study

• Graduate seminar course and the yearly presentation/report of research progress requirement.

• Successful public defense of a Master’s Thesis

• At least six (6) unbound thesis copies and CD-ROM copy of the approved Master’s thesis
