ACADEMICS

The UPMSI fosters aspiring marine scientists and professionals through advanced, multidisciplinary graduate programs. For research-oriented students, UPMSI grants M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Marine Science with different specializations. For professionals outside academia, it offers the Professional Masters in Tropical Marine Ecosystems Management (PM-TMEM) program, with an initial focus on Marine Protected Areas (MPAs). Graduate students receive support in the form of research assistantships and scholarships.

COURSES OFFERED
The UP Marine Science Institute graduate curriculum trains prospective marine scientists in the major divisions of marine science: marine biology, marine physical sciences and marine biotechnology. Core courses include Ocean Physics and Chemistry, the four disciplines of Oceanography, and Marine Biotechnology.
