PM-TMEM PROGRAM

As an accelerated program designed for professionals engaged in the management and government of tropical marine ecosystems, it integrates science and experience in addressing social and ecological concerns. It focuses on biodiversity, marine conservation, and sustainable development of the Coral Triangle region.

HOW TO APPLY

ADMISSION

• Applicants must meet the general requirements for admission to graduate school of the University.

• Must have a minimum of two (2) years work experience relevant to TMEM or Coastal Resource Management

• Must be practitioners in either of the two fields

APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS

• Curriculum vitae

• Official transcript of records from all colleges and universities attended

• Certified copies of diplomas or certificate graduation

• Birth certificate from Philippine Statistics Authority (For Filipino citizens)

• Two recommendation letters sealed in an envelope

• English Proficiency Test scores of 550 for TOEFL PBT, 79 for TOEFL iBT, and 6.5 for IELTS (For foreigners)
