MSI COURSES OFFERED

The UP Marine Science Institute graduate curriculum trains prospective marine scientists in the major divisions of marine science: marine biology, marine physical sciences and marine biotechnology. Core courses include Ocean Physics and Chemistry, the four disciplines of Oceanography, and Marine Biotechnology.

The UPMSI also offers undergraduate courses such as Oceans, the Marine Sciences, and Oceans and Us. Each student designs his or her own program of study, with interesting electives offered by the Institute and by other units of the College of Science. This curriculum adequately prepares graduate students to pursue their chosen fields of research.
